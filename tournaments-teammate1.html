Personal information

My full name is Nguyen Nhat Quang and my student ID is 3863937. My date of birth is May, 2nd 2001 in Vietnam so now I'm 19 years old. I graduated from Tran Phu high school in 2018 and now studying at RMIT University. My major is IT (Information Technology), this is my first semester. There are two languages that I can speak, Vietnamese and English; my main language is Vietnamese and I can use it fluently, however, English is always my nightmare, it can be said that I completely hate language subject. It has never been my ally and I always got bad marks in these subjects. At the present, I'm living in Ho Chi Minh city, however, my hometown is Tay Ninh.

About my hobby, I always spend my free time reading novels (Especially Chinese novels) listening to music(Jazz and classical) and playing video games(MMORPG game or some kind of card game). There is a quite interesting about me which is my name. My name means "Sunlight" and it exactly coincides with the name of a brand of dishwashing liquid 'Sunlight'. My friends always use my name as a joke, and I don't know this is a curse or not, because I'm always a person who has to wash dishes at every party. However, I don't care about that either, because 'Sunshine' is always the most important thing people need.

The answers to the reason why I want to study IT. IT has many parts that interest me in it. However, I can answer what impressed me the most with IT is its 'magic' ability. IT can connect people around the world by creating a bridge-like social network (Facebook, Instagram, etc.) or it can allow people to create a new world, like the hypothetical world in the series game Assassin's Creed or a simulation of the New York City in GTA V. Because of this 'miracle', that create the difference of the IT industry compared to other industries, the suitable example is the 'hacking' only IT is the only industry that can create two enemies with none-differences (information thief and security guard) with the same weapon (code and data). There may be many people who say that business can be a profession that creates similar competition, but in my opinion, it is not the same. In business, external factors like relationships affect a lot, but with IT differently, hacker teams and security and administration systems fight each other, the result can only be based on skills have any external factors.

About my IT experience, I have been in contact with computers since I was very young, I remember 5 or 6 years old. As I got older, I gradually realized that I have a passion for technology, especially programming. However, in the year I was in 9th grade (2015), I was playing League of Legends, and I was very angry with the characters at that time. I started thinking and realized that I wanted to create my champions, I wanted to create a game of my own. I started learning by myself, doing my own research and I see IT as a perfect start as well as a way back for me if I want to become a game designer. It can be said that League of Legends is the most important factor that makes me realize my wishes.

Career Plans

Like Assignment 1 and Assignment 2, in Assignment 3 my selection remains unchanged. I want to be a game developer, and in the distant future, I want to have a personal game studio. Looking at the planner boards, I can see that everyone on our team needs to learn how to code and develop apps for our purpose, though everyone has their choices for a different future such as a game developer, game programmer, programmer, or network security specialist. We have identified the path we need to take and are clearly aware of what we must overcome, what we must face. However, at this moment our goal is to create the perfect Magnum Opus.

The Thing that Assist My In My Career Plan

There is no denying that this project helped me a lot, there are three main reasons for this. First is the most important reason, it gives me a certain experience for the initial game launch, conceptualization, gameplay, role books, and the most basic designs. It gives me the experience and vision to apply to my future projects, giving me an easier start, avoiding a lot of roundabouts. The second reason is the ability to work in groups, this project allows me to work and collaborate with people with great knowledge and skills. They showed me my weaknesses, helped me change and develop myself, they showed me the effectiveness of teamwork (work speed and quality of work almost tripled to quadruple compared to when I did it alone). Last but not least, the technologies and skills used in this project can greatly help my future, I want to become a game developer so a lot of knowledge about the game, and the skills and technologies used to develop and operate the game. All of them appear in this project, it can be said that my job is just absorbing and changing.
