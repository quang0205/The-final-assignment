Project Description

  • The passions, interests, and skills of my group:

    • We found each other through the name - The Fat Guys. At first, we thought this was a group of members who were very different in personality, interests, and found each other for one goal – to have teammates. However, after quite a few days of working and talking to each other, we realized that we are extremely suitable for each other, from personality to hobby. First, speaking of personality, we feel very happy with each other's way of talking and sense of humor. In terms of interests, we all enjoy board games, online games and Japanese culture together. Therefore, our final decision was a card game called Magnum Opus with selective gameplay. Magnum Opus is a turn-based game card that follows the styles of the world's diverse cultures, and with exciting gameplay, we hope Magnum Opus's result is as perfect as its name titled.

    Although this card game is not finished, but I believe with passion and cohesion, we will devote our enthusiasm to develop this card game.

    In this course, completing a card game is an unbelievably difficult task. Therefore, for this assignment, we would like to present the site to the user in an inclusive way, while also detailed. Besides, we will also try to develop the interface of the game.

    To make a complete game card with gameplay that attracts users, we need to have full knowledge about IT fields such as coding, artificial intelligence,... planning and design. And The Fat Guys has quite a few elements. Nguyen - also Maple - is currently the leader of the group. Nguyen has contributed greatly in giving ideas about the project. In addition, Nguyen has many years of experience working with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Besides, our group also has other members such as Quang, Duc, Dai and Nham are also quite proficient in coding, have basic knowledge in IT fields such as AI, machine learning,... and teamwork. Besides, Dai and Nham also have the ability to design, especially Dai. Although we are just newbies in the IT world, we believe that we will strive constantly to improve ourselves and this unique idea – Magnum Opus.

  • IT industry trends:

    • In the present time, the IT industry is growing more than ever, more and more fields are applying as many technologies as possible. Specifically, in 2020, IT has been applied a lot in the field of medicine and education due to the appearance of COVID, other fields such as online games are also thriving during this period due to the needs of people while in quarantine. The development direction of IT stretches throughout many fields. Top technology trends that are being developed by 2021 include:

    • AI and Machine Learning: the development and investment in AI and machine learning is no longer a thing to discuss at this time.
    • Robotic Process Automation - RPA : use software for automatic processes such as transaction processing, data processing or Gmail replying.
    • Virtual reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR) and Extended Reality (XR): there are specific applications such as in education, entertainment, surgeon training, for museum visitors to have deep experiences.
    • Internet of Things (IoT)
    • 5G: after 3G and 4G, 5G is a technology that is expected to revolutionize us.
    • Cybersecurity: now the need to increase security is becoming more necessary than ever. So we believe that this is an area that will grow extremely strong in the coming years. And a variety of other technologies are also being developed in the present time.
